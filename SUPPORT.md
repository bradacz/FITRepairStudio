# Support

## Community Support

Use GitHub issues for:

- Reproducible bugs.
- FIT files that fail to parse or repair, after removing private data.
- Feature requests.
- Documentation fixes.

App source repository: https://github.com/bradacz/FITRepairStudio

## Private Contact

For private support, contact:

```text
info@mylocalio.com
```

Do not send private activity files unless they are necessary for diagnosis and
you are comfortable sharing them with Localio Labs s.r.o.

## Voluntary Support

FIT Repair Studio is free and open source. Voluntary contributions help cover
Apple Developer Program membership, notarized builds, hosting, and maintenance.

- Buy Me a Coffee: https://www.buymeacoffee.com/mariantomay
- Website donation form: https://fitrepairstudio.site/

Payment is optional and does not unlock extra features.
